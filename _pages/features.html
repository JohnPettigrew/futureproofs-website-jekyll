What can Futureproofs do for you?

Clear, powerful, practical tools

Easy to learn

Futureproofs uses established markup standards (BSI and Chicago) to make it quick to learn and to minimise the amount of data entry needed. There are no clumsy buttons or toolbars â€“ just draw on the screen as you would on paper and Futureproofs gives you the markup you need. Get comprehensive help, including guides to getting started.

Real PDF proofing

Unlike other PDF software, Futureproofs is designed specifically for the jobs editors, proofreaders, authors and project managers to every day. Upload your PDFs and be confident that what you see on screen matches what will be printed - but experience the freedom to mark up the files easily and consistently.

All the tools you need

Work faster wherever you are to catch mistakes more easily. Futureproofs lets you work with text or artworks, in mono or colour, regardless of the page-layout system you use. Intelligent search and navigation tools, dedicated review modes for different user roles, and the easiest markup in the industry.

Secure, time-saving collaboration

Work together

Create project teams, communicate clearly and monitor their progress to manage the whole team effectively. Review the team's markup and quickly collate their input into a single master proof.

Hosted in the cloud

Browser-based and cloud-hosted, Futureproofs needs no installation and is always up to date, wherever you are. Work on your proofs wherever you have internet.

Role-based access

Choose who can access your content and data, and how much information each person can see. Add and remove people from teams, change roles and ensure people have the tools they need (and no confusing extras).

Insight, control and problem-avoidance

Real-time insight

Always know what's going on with your projects so you can deal with issues before they become problems. Each project has a dashboard showing up-to-the-minute statistics for every member of the team - time spent working, corrections made, how many queries they're handling etc.

Project control in your hands

Real-time data puts control in your hands. See who's done their work, whether the schedule is slipping, or if extra changes are being made. Find out who made what change and when. Review conversations and decisions.

Detect and avoid problems

Quickly detect whether work will be done on time and intervene. Control budgets with clear insight into supplier activity. Avoid expensive re-work and prevent changes being made too late in the process.
