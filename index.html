A better book workflow

You've spent years building your publishing process. But now, you need to go on-screen.

Futureproofs provides the answer. Built on decades of real-world editorial and production experience, Futureproofs solves the day-to-day problems of on-screen proof checking.

We know why editorial and production teams work the way they do, and we've built software that really does help them work faster and better. We know what managers need, and we give you the tools to manage your team without constant check-ins, emails or meetings. Futureproofs provides the tools and training for your team to deliver!

The power to publish faster

Your team will spend less time on their proofs, less time chasing progress, less time reading emails and less time worrying about different versions of the project. Markup in Futureproofs is up to 25% faster than other PDF software, while creating master proofs and comparing different versions are up to TWICE as fast as working on paper. We help streamline workflows and eliminate re-work and unnecessary emails. Plus, markup in Futureproofs is always clear and consistent, to eliminate miscommunication!
